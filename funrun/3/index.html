About The Event

A city run built like a night headline.

Neon Dash 5K turns Manila Bay into a kinetic race corridor: sharp lights, loud beats, flat pavement, and a crowd that keeps the pace alive from warmup to finish chute.

Why it hits different

  • Sunrise start along the bayfront with a fully closed road section.
  • Live MC, music zones, and a post-race party built into the finish area.
  • Open, competitive, and kids categories in one event village.
