Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Your registration includes a race bib, finisher medal (for the first 50 finishers), event T-shirt, race kit bag, timing chip, and access to the post-race party with food and live music. Water stations are provided throughout the course.

Absolutely! The 5K Fun Run category is designed for all fitness levels. The route is completely flat along Manila Bay Boulevard with no elevation changes. There is no cut-off time for the Fun Run category — run, jog, or walk at your own pace.

Yes, everyone is welcome! The Fun Run category has no requirements regarding pace or running experience. We encourage first-timers to join. Just make sure you're in reasonably good health — if you have any medical conditions, please consult your doctor beforehand.

Wear your event T-shirt, comfortable running shoes, and athletic bottoms. We encourage neon colors and glow accessories to match the Neon Dash theme — but there is no strict dress code. We'll have a glow station at the assembly area with light accessories for participants!

Race kit claiming is on June 12–13, 2025 (2:00PM – 8:00PM) at SM Mall of Asia Activity Center, Pasay City. On race day (June 14), late kit claiming will be available from 3:30AM–5:00AM at Tent A near the start line. Bring a valid ID and your registration confirmation email.

The event is rain or shine! Light to moderate rain will not cancel the race. In case of a severe weather advisory (Signal No. 1 or higher), the event organizers will make an official announcement via our social media pages no later than 4:00AM on race day. Registered participants will be informed by email as well.
