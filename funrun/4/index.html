About The Run

A bright bayfront race with magazine-cover energy.

Neon Dash 5K brings sunrise light, music, and fast flat pavement together for a race morning that feels polished from warmup through finish chute. It is built for first-timers, competitive runners, and families who want a sharper event experience.

Why It Stands Out

  • Sunrise start along Manila Bay with warm, open views.
  • On-course music zones and a post-race celebration village.
  • Fast support setup with hydration, medics, and clean route signage.
